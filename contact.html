Frequently Asked Questions

Is ProxyWarp really free?
Yes, completely. No ads, no account required, no paywalls. We're kept alive by generous donations.
Do you log my browsing activity?
No. We do not store browsing history, IP addresses, or any data about what you access. See our Privacy Policy.
Why doesn't a specific site work?
Some sites block all known proxy IPs, or require login that can't be proxied. Email us with the site URL and we'll look into it.
How do I submit a DMCA takedown?
Email contact@rukkit.net with "DMCA" in the subject line. Include the URL, a description of the content, and proof of ownership. See our DMCA Policy.
